Hello and welcome to my art website!

I am an aspiring computer programmer having returned from my placement year. During that time I have worked as a PHP and Java developer, working on bug fixes for our company product. The company product was divided into two seperate sites: one customer facing, and the other was used by Service Desk and 75% of our clients. I was assigned to the team fixing and adding features to the latter. I have learned to use Docker, Jira, RabbitMQ, SonarQube, SOAPUI and MySQL Workbench on my placement, and worked predominantly with the Symfony PHP framework. After returning from placement I felt like I needed a new challenge for my final year project. I have chosen front-end web development as a second year module, and I also wanted a frontend final year project as a way to show off my creative mind. The subject of algorithmic and generative art was introduced to me at university, and I was already fascinated by mandalas and the dot-work tattoo style. Following the end of university, I will be adding artwork to this site in my own time. The pieces I have developed during university time are:

  • Custom Fractal Builder
  • L-system garden
  • L-system mandala
  • L-system DNA

The basis for my final year project is L-systems or Lindenmayer systems, an algorithm that allows the final drawn product to exhibit self similarity to its smaller components. After taking this project further in my spare time, I will add a range of additional algorithms that can be used to generate artwork.
