L-System DNA double helix

The work Aristid Lindenmayer conducted on plant biology inspired me further. Following the creation of the L-system garden, I found evidence of the existence of L-systems in the construction of proteins and DNA from nucleotides. The user is prompted with fewer inputs, however the artwork has a more "show your working" feel to demonstrate the existence of the L-system. This has a similar mechanism to the Custom Fractal Builder, however each nucleotide base will only call two others.

In terms of ATGC, which bases are called following a base being added into the helix? Start with an axiom using a single nucleotide base, the iteration system will concatenate a single DNA strand based on the iteration system. A second strand will then be constructed based on the matching nucleotide. For best results, input 2-4 bases.
